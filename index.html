Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a tutor?

Simply Sign up, complete your profile, and apply as a tutor. Our team will verify your credentials before approval.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes! Students can access free tutoring sessions. However, premium tutors offer paid sessions for deeper learning.

How do i book a tutoring session?

Go to the 'Find a Tutor' page, select a tutor, and choose a time slot that works for you.

Can I cancel or reschedule my session?

Yes, you can manage your bookings in the 'My Bookings' section.
